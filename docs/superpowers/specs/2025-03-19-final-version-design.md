# Abidostransport Final Version Design

## Goal
Get more customers through an optimized website and Facebook presence.

## Website Enhancements

### 1. Online Quote Calculator
- Instant price estimates based on distance and vehicle type
- Quebec/Ontario rate charts
- Distance calculation between major cities
- "Get Exact Quote" CTA button

**Pricing Structure:**
- Montreal to Toronto: $400-700
- Montreal to Ottawa: $300-500
- Quebec City to Toronto: $500-800
- Cross-border (Florida): $1,200-1,800

**Vehicle Multipliers:**
- Sedan: 1.0x
- SUV/Crossover: 1.2x
- Truck/Pickup: 1.3x
- Luxury/Classic: 1.5x (enclosed required)

### 2. Customer Reviews Section
- Homepage testimonial carousel (existing)
- Dedicated reviews page with star ratings
- Location badges per customer
- Ready for Google Reviews integration

### 3. Service Area Map
- Visual coverage of Quebec and Ontario
- Major cities highlighted
- Cross-border routes to USA
- Click-to-call buttons per region

### 4. Conversion Optimizations
- Sticky "Call Now" button on mobile
- Click-to-call phone numbers throughout
- Trust badges (20+ years, insured, licensed)
- Urgency elements
