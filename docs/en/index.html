Frequently Asked Questions

How much does car transport cost in Canada?

Car transport costs vary based on distance, vehicle type, and transport method. Average cost from Montreal to Toronto is $400-$700. Get a free instant quote on our website.

How long does vehicle shipping take?

Local transport within Quebec or Ontario: 1-3 days. Cross-country shipping: 7-14 days. USA transport: 5-10 days depending on destination.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes! All vehicles are fully insured during transport. We provide up to $100,000 coverage at no extra cost.

Do you offer door-to-door service?

Yes, we offer convenient door-to-door pickup and delivery across Quebec, Ontario, and all Canadian provinces.

Can I ship my car to the USA?

Absolutely! We specialize in Canada-USA vehicle transport including all customs paperwork and cross-border documentation.

What vehicles do you transport?

We transport all vehicle types: sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, motorcycles, classic cars, luxury vehicles, and oversized vehicles.
